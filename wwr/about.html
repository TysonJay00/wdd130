History

White Water Rafting was just recently founded this year by a man with a dream. Tyson Wilde grew up in southern Alberta and ventured out to the mountains every year to experience the wilderness, as well as both it's calm rivers, and it's more rapid waters.

Wilde's dreams are to show people from around the world, Canada's most beautful sights while experiencing a natural thrilling ride, or calming rafting on the Bow River. Wilde searched for the best waters to take both adults and children out to Alberta's treasured rocky mountains.
